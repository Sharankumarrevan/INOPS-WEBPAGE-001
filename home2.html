About InOps

InOps is a leading workforce and contractor lifecycle management (CLMS) platform designed to simplify compliance, automate operations, and enhance workforce visibility across industrial and enterprise environments.
At InOps, we help organizations eliminate manual processes and transition to fully automated contractor and workforce management systems. Our platform integrates biometric devices, access control systems, HRMS, payroll, and compliance tracking into one unified ecosystem.

Our Mission

To empower businesses with intelligent automation that ensures compliance, improves operational efficiency, and provides real-time workforce insights for better decision-making.
 Contact Us

What We Offer

Comprehensive solutions designed to automate contractor management, enhance security, and ensure regulatory compliance across your organization.

Contractor Labour Management System (CLMS)

End-to-end contractor compliance automation

Digitize onboarding, documentation verification and compliance approvals. Track contractor lifecycle, automate statutory workflows and reduce compliance risks across projects.

Biometric & Access Control

Secure workforce access

Integration with biometric devices, facial recognition and turnstile access control. Real-time attendance syncing with centralized dashboards and automated security alerts.

Analytics & Compliance Reporting

Data-driven insights & compliance

Department-wise, shift-wise and plant-wise real-time dashboards. Gain actionable insights with audit-ready reports and predictive workforce analytics.
